About

I am a certified GIS professional (GISP) with experience in geospatial data management, remote sensing, and web-based GIS application development. I am interested in creating accessible GIS platforms, developing applications that make a positive difference on communities and the environment, and helping others do important work through GIS.

I currently serve as a GIS Specialist with the Indiana Geographic Information Office, where I help manage statewide geospatial data infrastructure, provide state agency GIS support, and manage the IndianaMap open data program.
